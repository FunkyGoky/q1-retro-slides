Stage 3 — Root cause
5
whys deep.

Pick the top theme. Ask why until you hit something real. Most retros stop at symptoms — we don't.

We're not solving yet. We're diagnosing.

Stage 4 — Decide actions

Every action needs three things

What
Specific. Observable. We'd know if it happened.
Who
One named owner. Not "the team."
When
A real date. By end of next sprint, by review day, by Friday.

Bias to fewer, sharper actions. Three good ones beat ten vague ones.

Look forward

What do you want for Q2?

One thing each. A feeling, a way of working, a goal.
